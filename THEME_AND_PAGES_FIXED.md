### About the 404 Errors

The 404 errors you see are **normal** and **expected** because:

1. **No data yet:** You haven't uploaded any videos or created posts
2. **API returns empty arrays:** When pages try to load data, they get empty results
3. **Pages handle this gracefully:** They show "No videos yet" or "No posts yet" messages

**This is NOT a bug** - it's how the app works when starting fresh.

Once you:
- Upload videos → Videos page will show them
- Create posts → Posts page will show them
- Add accounts → Accounts page will show them
